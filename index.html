Use Cases

What we've built — and what we build for you

AI isn't one-size-fits-all. Below are the kinds of workflows we design, build, and deploy for teams that want real automation — not a chatbot demo.

Legal

Contract Review & Due Diligence

Automatically flag non-standard clauses, extract key dates and obligations, and surface risk across hundreds of documents in minutes instead of days.

e.g. M&A due diligence: classify 2,000 vendor contracts by risk tier overnight.

Finance

Document Processing & Compliance Checks

Extract structured data from earnings reports, loan applications, or regulatory filings — and run rule-based compliance checks automatically before human review.

e.g. Mortgage pipeline: pull income, liabilities, and red flags from uploaded docs, pre-scored for underwriters.

Healthcare

Clinical & Administrative Automation

Summarize patient intake forms, draft prior-authorization letters, or route inbound messages to the right care team — with audit trails and human-in-the-loop checkpoints built in.

e.g. Specialty clinic: reduce admin staff time on prior-auth letters by 70%.

Operations

Internal Knowledge & Process Automation

Turn your SOPs, runbooks, and internal wikis into an AI that answers employee questions, routes tickets, and escalates edge cases — without hallucinating policies that don't exist.

e.g. Logistics company: HR & IT helpdesk bot trained on internal docs, with verified citations on every answer.

E-commerce & Retail

Product Data & Customer Workflows

Auto-generate and enrich product listings, triage customer support tickets by intent and urgency, or analyze return patterns to flag supplier issues early.

e.g. Marketplace seller: bulk-generate SEO-ready listings for 5,000 SKUs from raw supplier data.

Research & Reports

Market Research & Report Generation

Gather, synthesize, and structure information from multiple sources into polished reports — with citations, summaries, and custom formatting that matches your brand.

e.g. Consulting firm: first-pass competitive analysis drafted in 20 minutes, ready for analyst review.

How We Work

A process built for real workflows

Most AI projects fail at deployment, not at the demo. We're built around making production the default.

1
Understand your workflow first

We start by mapping what actually happens in your process — not by showing you a generic AI demo and asking you to fit into it.

2
Build a scoped prototype fast

A working prototype on real data, scoped tightly. You see output in days, not months, so feedback is grounded in actual results.

3
Evaluate and harden

We run evals against your edge cases, add human-in-the-loop checkpoints where needed, and instrument the system before it touches real users.

4
Ship and monitor

Deploy into your environment, with logging and alerting from day one. We stay on for the first run and remain available as your workflow evolves.
